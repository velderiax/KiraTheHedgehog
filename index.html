How to Buy

Follow these easy steps to become a KIRA holder

1. Connect Wallet

Use a Solana-compatible wallet such as Phantom or Solflare. Ensure you're connected to the Solana network.

2. Get $SOL

Buy or deposit $SOL from exchanges like Binance or Coinbase into your wallet to prepare for swapping.

3. Swap $SOL to $XIAN

Use Raydium to swap $SOL to $XIAN.

4. Bridge $XIAN to Mainnet

Go to bridge.xian.org to bridge your $XIAN from Solana to Mainnet.

5. Swap for $KIRA

Use SNAKexchange or another Solana DEX to trade for $KIRA.
